About Us

Lucrative Financial Solutions

Lucrative Solutions is one of India’s well recognized and respected stockbroking and Wealth Management Companies and is known for progressive strategies throughout various trading platforms. Lucrative Solutions is run by a set of experienced Professionals and thrives on providing “Real Value for money” to a diversified client base. We operate on a unique retail focused stock trading model that provides revolutionary trading platforms. We are dedicated to serve individuals and corporate segments with a customer-centric model and custom designed investments through financial products such as equity, derivatives, mutual funds, insurance, portfolio management, financial planning, wealth management, and much more by serving them at their doorstep. As one of the leading wealth managers we are a member of the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE), National Stock Exchange (NSE) in the country, we additionally oversee compliance by trading and clearing members and listed companies with the rules and regulations of the exchange.

We are a first generation wealth management company headquartered in Bangalore, India. The company is founded by Abhishek Rangaswamy and is a nationally acclaimed leading stock broking and wealth management firm.
